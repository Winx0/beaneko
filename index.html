About Beaneko

Beaneko is not just another meme token. Born from the combination of beans and neko (cat), this project combines fun with real utility in the cryptocurrency space.

Our mission is to build a strong community of holders and supporters while providing unique utility in the meme token landscape.

With a dedicated team of developers and marketers, Beaneko aims to become a staple name in the meme coin space, bringing both entertainment and value to our holders.

Key Features

  • Community-driven governance
  • Regular token burns to increase scarcity
  • Unique rewards for long-term holders
  • Strong liquidity locking to ensure stability
